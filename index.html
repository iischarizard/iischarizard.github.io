ABOUT

WHAT IS HACK SOCAL AND WHAT IS OUR VISION?

Hack SoCal, Southern California’s first co-ed high school hackathon, was founded on the notion to bring opportunities for students in Southern California to become successful in the Computer Science field. As a student-run hackathon, we aim for our overnight event to be an awesome experience that enables aspiring programmers to meet others with the same passion. Students are also encouraged to build on soft skills such as teamwork, leadership, time management, public speaking, etc.

Driving 6+ hours to attend a hackathon in Silicon Valley is not a piece of cake for those of us located in the Los Angeles area, so Hack SoCal is aiming to bring a more localized hackathon for SoCal high schoolers to attend!

FAQ

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24-hour event where a large number of people meet to engage in collaborative computer programming. They reach out to all college and high school students, especially those looking into becoming a computer science major.


What is Hack SoCal?

Hack SoCal was founded as Southern California’s first student-run, co-ed high school hackathon. The event lasts 24-hours, overnight as a targeted number of 150 attendees work to build a software project that addresses a real-world problem. Our mission is to encourage students within the Southern California area to pursue computing and technology and promote diversity in this ever-growing field.

Do I need to know how to code?

No, you do not need to know how to speak in computer dialects. There will be mentors present to teach you.


When will applications be opened and when will it close?

Applications open in September. We will be closing applications the week before our event.


Can I use something I hacked before the event?

Unfortunately, you can't. Please hold off on hacking until after the event begins if you plan on submitting your creation.

Where do I sleep?

We have designated rooms for you to nap in, don't worry!

Who can attend?

Students that are attending a high school in the United States are more than welcome to attend! If you are not attending high school, we highly encourage you to apply as a volunteer or mentor.


Will you provide travel reimbursements?

Travel reimbursements will not be provided, but we still encourage you to attend for the experience!



How much does it cost??

It won’t cost anything at all! Everything will be provided, and you will also be taking home some cool SWAG given by sponsors!

What should I bring?

You should bring a laptop, school ID, and chargers. However, sleeping bags, toiletries, and anything you might want is recommended!


How large can a team be?

Each team is composed of 4 people. You can either bring a team of 4, or team up with new hackers! We highly encourage meeting new people :)
