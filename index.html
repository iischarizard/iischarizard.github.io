ABOUT

WHAT IS HACK SOCAL AND WHAT IS OUR VISION?

Hack SoCal, Southern California’s first co-ed high school hackathon, was founded on the notion to bring opportunities for students in Southern California to become successful in the Computer Science field. As a student-run hackathon, we aim for our overnight event to be an awesome experience that enables aspiring programmers to meet others with the same passion. Students are also encouraged to build on soft skills such as teamwork, leadership, time management, public speaking, etc.

Driving 6+ hours to attend a hackathon in Silicon Valley is not a piece of cake for those of us located in the Los Angeles area, so Hack SoCal is aiming to bring a more localized hackathon for SoCal high schoolers to attend!

FAQ

A hackathon is a coding "competition" where hackers race to complete their own product, website, game, app, or piece of hardware to win prizes, meet new friends, and learn.

Prizes

There's ton of things to win at MontyHacks! Our prize categories are : Environmental, Health/Fitness, Education, Game, and Mobile Application!


Who can attend?

Any student who will be in high school for the 2017-2018 school year can apply! If you are an exception, please contact us.


How much does it cost?

It won’t cost anything at all! Everything will be provided, and you will also be taking home some cool SWAG given by sponsors!!


Can I use something I hacked before the event?

Unfortunately, you can't. Please don't start your hack until after the ceremony if you plan to submit it.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, phone, chargers for both, and any form of identification. You may also want headphones, a mouse, or any other electronic accessory that makes you more comfortable in a working environment.


When will applications be open?

Applications are open!


How will the selection process go?

Hackers will be reviewed based on their application, with regards to experience, relative interest, etc. Note that we are trying to accept everybody who registers and accomodate for those coming as a a team.

Do I need to join a team in order to apply/hack?

No, there will be time for team formation at the beginning of the event. Solo hackers are equally welcome!


How large can a team be?

Each team is composed of 4 people. You can either bring a team of 4, or team up with new hackers! We highly encourage meeting new people :)


Will you provide travel reimbursement?

Travel reimbursements will not be provided, but we still encourage you to attend for the experience!


Do I need to know how to code?

No, you do not need to know how to speak in computer. There will be mentors present to teach you.
